# Knob — Survey Findings
May 9, 2026
**Respondents:** 20 parents of Class 12 students

## Key Findings

- 45% had missed a college admission deadline before
- 75% find tracking multiple colleges difficult
- 8/20 prefer automated mobile or email alerts
- Only 4/20 (20%) willing to pay directly

## Critical Insight

B2C monetisation is weak. Flipped to B2B model:
- Free for parents
- Colleges pay per admission season
- Colleges get access to intent-based leads from
  parents who already shortlisted them
