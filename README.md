# Knob — Admission Alert App
### A PM Case Study by Sushanth D

## The Problem
Parents of Class 12 students manually check 
3-5 college websites repeatedly during admission 
season — missing deadlines, causing anxiety, and 
wasting hours on information that hasn't changed.

## The Solution
Knob is a personalised admission alert app. 
Parents select colleges they want to track and 
get notified the moment admissions open — with 
a direct link to apply.

## Research
- 20 parents surveyed — May 2026
- 45% had missed a deadline before
- 75% find multi-college tracking difficult
- 8/20 prefer automated alerts

## Business Model
B2B — Free for parents, colleges pay per admission season for access to intent-based leads from parents who already shortlisted them.

## Prototype
Built on Lovable — 4 screens:
- Onboarding (stream selector + college search)
- Dashboard (live status tracking)
- Alert (admission open notification)
- College Admin (B2B lead preview)
